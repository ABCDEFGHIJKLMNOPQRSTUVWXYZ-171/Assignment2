Budgeting is essential for financial stability during university. Start by tracking your expenses and identifying where your money goes. Meal planning, avoiding impulse buys, and using apps like Mint or YNAB can help you save and plan more efficiently.

Successful students use proven study habits. Spaced repetition, active recall, and focused sessions (without distractions) are highly effective. Don't forget to take regular breaks, stay hydrated, and sleep well to maximize your academic potential.
